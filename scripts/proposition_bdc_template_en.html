Late Payment Penalties

Any late payment automatically triggers, from the day following the due date:

  • late interest at the ECB rate plus 10 percentage points (art. L. 441-10 French Commercial Code);
  • statutory flat indemnity of forty (40) euros for collection costs (art. D. 441-5 French Commercial Code);
  • contractual flat indemnity of two hundred and fifty (250) euros per overdue invoice.

These penalties are cumulative. If a payment is more than ten (10) days overdue, Lucid-Lab may suspend all Services and declare immediate repayment of all outstanding amounts.
